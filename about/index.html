El Bachir Outidrarine

I am Full Stack Software Engineer/UX Designer

I’ve loved helping others and solving problems for as long as I can remember, and I know it’s the general path I want to pursue.
Growing up, I had never considered pursuing computer science. Aside from my schools’ unfortunate lack of CS courses, I always pictured computer science as a diffuclt field, and that you can have no social interaction in the CS industry. But my curiosity turned me to be a software engineer.
I started my journey as a self-thought, using online tools and various books. Since English was not my first language, the road was a bit rough. Then after getting my High School Degree, I chose to continue my studies in Computer Sciences at the Agadir Higher School of Technology.
Currently, I am pursuing a DUT diploma in Computer Science.
